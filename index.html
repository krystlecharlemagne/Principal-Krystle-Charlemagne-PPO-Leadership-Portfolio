üéØ Vision & Purpose

At P.S./I.S. 66, all students will become confident, proficient, and joyful readers and writers through rigorous instruction, targeted intervention, and continuous progress monitoring within a collaborative MTSS framework.

üìã MTSS Core Principles

‚úì All students can achieve grade-level literacy standards with timely support.

‚úì Instruction and intervention are proactive, preventive, and data-driven.

‚úì Supports are flexible and intensify based on student need.

‚úì Implementation occurs schoolwide and is grounded in evidence-based practices.

‚úì Collaboration among teachers, leaders, and families ensures equitable outcomes.

üìö Literacy Priorities

Focus Area Schoolwide Strategy Evidence of Implementation

Frequency: Daily

Lead Staff: Classroom Teachers

Tier 2: Targeted Support

Description: Targeted small-group instruction for students below 40th percentile.

Frequency: 3√ó/week

Lead Staff: ELA Intervention Team

Resources: i-Ready Lessons, Phonics Boost, Reading Strategies Toolkit

Tier 3: Intensive Intervention

Description: Intensive individualized intervention for students below 20th percentile.

Frequency: 4‚Äì5√ó/week

Lead Staff: AP Oversight + Interventionists

Resources: LLI, 1:1 Reading Conference, Targeted Comprehension Practice
